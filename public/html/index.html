Raise Your Standards of Living

Self-catering apartments are located near the Old town area, in the center of Tallinn, in the area of cultural and historic value named Kassisaba, at Roopa Street house no 19/1. Apartments offer a good affordable accomodation for private traveller or family lodging in owners house. These are owners apartments and therefore offered without mediators and dealers. The house with apartments lies near the district of British and Danish Embassies (4 minutes) and just 10 minutes walk from the Parliament Building in Old Town. It takes 3 minutes to walk to the modern shopping center to buy groceries or other necessary products. Nearest fitness and spa facilities 4 minutes to walk. Neighbourhood is very quiet, despite the fact that the location is central. So if You like to have a good night sleep after a city-break, this is the place for You.
