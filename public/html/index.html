Porject Description


The family summer camp project is specialized to provide guidance for Chinese high school students or college students who are planning to go abroad for advanced education. The enrolled students can gain an opportunity to have a one-month trip to the United States accompanied by a parent. While having English courses taught by local teachers in our west coast base camp, the students can stay with local families to get familiar with the American culture and prepare themselves for future study in the United States.
